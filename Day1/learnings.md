## 📌 What is Salesforce?

Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform used to manage customer relationships, sales, support, and business operations.

### Key Points
- Cloud-based platform
- Used by companies worldwide
- Helps manage customers and sales
- Supports automation and reporting

---

## 📌 What is CRM?

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.

It is used to:
- Store customer information
- Track customer interactions
- Improve customer service
- Manage sales and support activities

### Benefits of CRM
- Better customer communication
- Organized customer data
- Increased productivity
- Improved business growth

---

## 📌 What is an Object in Salesforce?

An Object in Salesforce is similar to a database table used to store information.

### Object Contains
- Fields → Columns
- Records → Rows

### Example
Student Object:
- Name
- Roll Number
- Branch

### Standard Salesforce Objects
- Account
- Contact
- Lead
- Opportunity

---

## 📌 How to Create Trailhead Playground

### Steps
1. Open Salesforce Trailhead
2. Sign in to your account
3. Click profile icon
4. Select Hands-on Orgs
5. Click Create Playground
6. Wait for setup completion

Trailhead Playground is used for practice and learning Salesforce.
