# Day 1 Notes

## What is Salesforce?
Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform used to manage customers, sales, marketing, and support.

## What is CRM?
CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.

It helps companies manage customer information and interactions.

## Salesforce Objects
Objects are like database tables.

Examples:
- Lead
- Account
- Contact
